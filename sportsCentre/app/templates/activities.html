Activities

Step into our sports center and embark on a journey of self-discovery, where you'll
find the strength, motivation, and support you need to achieve your fitness goals.

Swimming

There are three types of swimming activities offered in most swimming pools, which are general use, lane swimming, and swimming lessons.

General use allows swimmers to freely swim and play in the pool, while lane swimming is for those who want to swim laps and improve their technique. Swimming lessons are available for beginners and those looking to improve their skills.

You can also participate in our team events!

Fitness

Our fitness offers various types of exercise equipment to help people improve their fitness and health.

Some common exercise machines include treadmills, stationary bikes, elliptical machines, rowming machines, weightlifting machines, and free weights.

General use allows customer to freely using machines.

Squash

We have 2 large courts. Squash is typically played with two people, but some cases, double matches can also be played.

With 2 squash courts available and 4 players per court, up to 8 people can play simultaneously.

Sports hall

You can play basketball, volleyball, dodgeball and other ball sports here, as well as jump rope and whatever you want.

We have team events on Thursday and Saturday, so check the timetable if you are interested!

Climbing

With state-of-the-art equipment and expert instructions, you will learn how to climb safely and effetively while getting a full-body workout.

We have a general class for every level!

Pilates

Our classis equipped with professional, mordern lecture halls and a variety of Pilates equipments, and provides personalised feedback.

Classes are only available on Mondays from 6-7pm.

Aerobics

You can perform to music and led by an instructor with members up to 25.

Music is always upbead and trendy.

You can pick the time between 10-11am on Tues, 7-8pm on Thurs, and 10-11 on Sat.

Yoga

A yoga class combines physical postures, breathing techniques, and meditation to promote physical and mental wellness.

It can improve flexibility, strength, balance, and reduce stress levels.

We have two classes 7-8pm on Friday and 9-10am on Sunday.
